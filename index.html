About Us

BS GREENSMART INVESTMENT LIMITED is committed to transforming agriculture in Kenya through safe agricultural practices leading to increased food productivity,environmental conservation,decent and fulfilling jobs for young women and men in the society. Based in Siaya County, we specialize in aquaculture, apiculture, Black Soldier Fly farming,Pig farming and Azolla cultivation. Our mission is to empower farmers, enhance food security, and create opportunities for youth through innovative farming and training services.
